Our Courses

BLIS - Bachelors in Library Science

Gain a comprehensive understanding of library and information science, digital archiving, and knowledge management. This program prepares you for roles in academic, public, and digital libraries, equipping you with cataloguing, research, and information technology skills.

Diploma in Oriental Librarianship

Specialize in the preservation and management of oriental manuscripts and literature. Learn about rare book handling, archival science, and the cultural significance of oriental collections, opening doors to unique opportunities in heritage institutions.

Accountancy of Panchayati Raj Institution

Master the principles of public sector accounting, financial management, and governance specific to Panchayati Raj Institutions. This course empowers you to contribute to transparent and efficient local governance and rural development.

Functional Arabic

Develop practical proficiency in Arabic for professional and academic contexts. The curriculum covers reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension, with a focus on real-world communication and cultural understanding.

Functional Persian

Acquire essential Persian language skills for business, research, and cultural exchange. This course emphasizes conversational fluency, script literacy, and the rich literary heritage of the Persian-speaking world.
