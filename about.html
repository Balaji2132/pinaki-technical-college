Our Story

Established in 2010, Pinaki Technical College began its journey with a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize quality technical education in Bihar. Founded by a group of dedicated educators and industry professionals, our institution was born out of the recognition that the region needed a world-class technical college that could compete with the best institutions nationally.

Located strategically at 139 NH, Arwal, Patna Main Road, our campus serves as a hub of learning and innovation. Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a modest beginning to become one of the most respected technical institutions in the region, graduating over 500 successful professionals who are now contributing to various industries across India and abroad.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our 95% placement rate and strong industry partnerships with over 50 leading companies. We take pride in our holistic approach to education that combines rigorous academic training with practical skills development, ensuring our students are not just job-ready but are future leaders in their fields.
